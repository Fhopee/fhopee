3. Packaging system composition and functional description:
3.1 Packaging system composition:
The vertical packaging system consists of turnstile, coil lifter 1, an automatic eye-through wrapping
packaging machine (with a trolley car), coil lifter 2, flipping machine, a discharge line 1 (with a
conveyor line on the flipping machine, a total of 2 buffer stations), a main electrical cabinet, an
operation cabinet, a hydraulic station, a maintenance rooftop and guardrail, a maintenance safety
ladder, etc. It can carry out operations such as picking, packaging, flipping and stacking steel strips

(1) Turnstile function: It can store the divided steel coils and cooperate with the coil lifter 1 for
picking-up and loading operations. The turnstile can carry 10T * 2 and has an arm length greater

operation, shuttling back and forth between the coil lifter, wrapping machine, and flipping machine,
connecting the three in series for online alternating operation.
