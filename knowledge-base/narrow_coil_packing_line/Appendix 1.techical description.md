5. Brief description of the technological process
The new conveyor-section line for the vertical-axis packaging of semi-
finished coils with a capacity of 80,000 t/y shall be intended for: receiving semi-
finished coils, tilting lightweight coils, centring, providing them with internal
packaging elements, moving the coils through the sections of the line with a
roller table, strapping and wrapping, weighing, and moving the coils with a
magnetic stacker to be discharged from the line using discharging cars.
