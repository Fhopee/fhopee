Продавцом.

консультационных услуг Покупатель сообщит
следующие данные по направляемому к
Продавцу персоналу: фамилия и имя, пол,
дата рождения, гражданство, специальность.
2.5.
The Seller shall submit the preliminary
consulting services program to the Buyer two
months before the consulting services start.
The consulting services program shall be finally
agreed by both parties within one month from
the date of the program submission by the
Seller.
One month before the start of the consulting
services, the Buyer shall inform the Seller of
name, sex, birth date, nationality, title of his
personnel.
